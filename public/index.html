Why Pupper?

Memes are great, but what if they could bring about real change?

Half of Pupper's supply is being donated to four exceptional non-profits, in real-time, spread out over the next 20 years. This is the first moment in history where supply and inflation are dictated entirely by charities.

We believe that by creating a community-driven token that supports and is intrinsically linked to non-profits over the long-term, we can all benefit.
